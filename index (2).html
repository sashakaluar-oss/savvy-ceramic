What is car paint protection? +
Car paint protection (also called ceramic coating) is a liquid polymer that bonds chemically to your paint, forming a hard, semi-permanent protective layer. It repels water, UV, bird droppings, tree sap, and road contaminants — keeping your car cleaner for longer. Our mobile service brings this protection directly to your driveway.
How long does ceramic coating last in Melbourne? +
3–5 years on a daily driver parked outside. 5–7+ years on a garaged vehicle. Melbourne's extreme UV and variable climate mean professional application is critical — DIY kits typically last under 12 months here.
