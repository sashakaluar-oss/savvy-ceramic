Our Commitment to You

THE SHELBY
WORK GUARANTEE

We stand behind every ceramic coating job we do. If something isn't right, we fix it — at our expense. Until it's fixed, you don't pay. Simple.

📞 0490 699 614 — Call Shelby Direct

The Shelby Work Guarantee is an important part of our customer service. If you have an issue with your service, please call us immediately on 0490 699 614. We will take it from there — quickly and fairly. Here is exactly what we commit to:

01

IF YOU'RE NOT SATISFIED — WE INSPECT AND FIX IT

If you are not satisfied with the job, contact us immediately on 0490 699 614. We will inspect the work and have any problems identified and fixed promptly.

If we believe the job is of good quality and you do not agree, we will jointly agree on an independent expert — a certified detailing professional — to inspect the vehicle and provide a written report.

This inspection is conducted at our expense. Any problems found by the expert will be corrected by Shelby Ceramic Coating.

Important: Until any problems identified are fixed, you are not required to pay for the job.

02

PROBLEMS NOT FIXED WITHIN A FORTNIGHT — FULL REFUND

We commit to resolving any identified problems within 14 days (a fortnight) of the inspection. If problems found by us or the independent expert are not fixed within this time frame, any advance payment made will be fully refunded.

Only if no problems are found by the independent expert will you be asked to pay the agreed price for the job, plus half the cost of the inspection report.

We are confident in our work quality — this process exists to protect you, and we have never needed to activate it.

03

IF THE JOB ISN'T DONE — YOUR MONEY BACK

If payment is made in advance and the job is not completed for any reason, the payment will be fully refunded. No questions, no delays.

We do not hold advance payments for work that is not delivered.

We accept deposits for bookings. If anything prevents us from completing your job, your deposit is returned in full.

CALL US NOW.

Shelby personally handles every guarantee enquiry. Call during business hours and we respond immediately.
