The Direct Answer: How Long Does Ceramic Coating Last?

A professional ceramic coating applied by Shelby Ceramic Coating in Clayton will last:

  • 3–5 years on a daily driver parked outside in Melbourne
  • 5–7 years on a daily driver with regular maintenance and occasional garage storage
  • 7+ years on a garage-kept weekend or show vehicle with proper care
  • 6–12 months for consumer-grade DIY ceramic coating kits in Melbourne's climate

These ranges are based on real-world results from our customers across the City of Monash — not manufacturer claims made in lab conditions.

Why Melbourne Specifically Matters

UV intensity: Melbourne regularly reaches UV Index 10–12+ in January and February — classified as Extreme. This is significantly higher than most European and North American cities where many ceramic coating durability claims originate. UV degrades both paint and coating, which is why Melbourne-specific application matters.

Acidic rain: Melbourne's rainfall contains atmospheric pollutants, particularly in urban and industrial areas like the Monash corridor. Acid rain actively attacks unprotected clear coat and low-quality coatings.

Temperature extremes: Melbourne can swing from 40°C summer heat to sub-10°C winter cold within days. These thermal cycles cause paint and coating to expand and contract. High-quality professional ceramic is formulated to handle this; cheaper consumer products are not.

Professional vs DIY Ceramic Coating in Melbourne

Consumer-grade ceramic coating kits use a lower concentration of SiO2 (silicon dioxide) and form a thinner, less durable layer. In Melbourne's climate, most DIY kits last 6–12 months before needing reapplication. Professional ceramic — like what we apply at Shelby — uses higher-concentration product requiring controlled environment application, specific curing conditions, and proper surface prep. This is why a professional application lasts years longer.

What Affects How Long Your Coating Lasts

Number of layers: Our Essential (1 layer) provides 2–3 year durability. Premium (2 layers) extends to 5 years. Ultimate (3 layers) to 7+ years.

Surface prep quality: A coating over contaminated or unpolished paint will delaminate faster. Perfect decontamination and paint correction (where needed) is essential for maximum longevity.

How you wash your car: pH-neutral car wash soap and a soft microfibre mitt preserves the coating. Automated car washes with harsh chemicals and stiff brushes are the single biggest thing that shortens ceramic coating life.

Parking exposure: A garage-kept car will retain coating significantly longer than one exposed to direct sun and rain 24/7.

How Do I Know When My Coating Has Worn Off?

The easiest test: pour water over your car's bonnet. If it beads into near-spherical droplets that roll straight off, the coating is active. If the water sheets across or puddles, the coating has degraded. Call Savvy on 0490 699 614 if you're unsure — we're happy to inspect and advise.

FAQ

Melbourne Durability — FAQ

Does Melbourne weather affect ceramic coating durability? +
Yes, significantly. Melbourne's extreme UV index, acidic rainfall, and temperature extremes degrade lesser coatings faster than milder climates. This is why professional-grade products and correct application are essential — and why DIY kits underperform here more than in cooler, less UV-intense cities.
How do I make my ceramic coating last longer? +
The single biggest thing: stop using automated car washes. Their harsh chemicals, rotating brushes, and high-pressure processes degrade ceramic coating faster than anything else. Use the two-bucket hand wash method with pH-neutral soap and a quality microfibre mitt. Apply a ceramic top-up spray every 6–12 months.
Can ceramic coating be reapplied or topped up? +
Yes. We offer maintenance inspections and top-up services. If your coating is showing early signs of degradation, we can apply a maintenance coat without full paint correction in many cases. Call Savvy on 0490 699 614 to discuss your situation.
