HOW WE DO

Proper Preparation

Ceramic coating is only as good as the preparation underneath it. Every job starts with a full decontamination wash, clay bar, and IPA wipe — no shortcuts.

02

Honest Advice

We'll tell you exactly what your paint needs before committing to any service. If paint correction is required, we'll say so — and explain why — before you spend a dollar.

We use professional-grade 9H ceramic products — not the consumer-grade kits sold at auto stores. The difference in longevity and quality is significant.
