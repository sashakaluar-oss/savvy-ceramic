The Process

HOW EVERY JOB
GETS DONE.

Whether we come to you or you visit our studio — every ceramic coating job follows the same meticulous process.

01

Book & Respond Fast

Submit your quote request online or call 0490 699 614. Shelby responds within 30 minutes — guaranteed. We discuss your car, your goals, and lock in the best approach.

02

Wash & Decontamination

Every job starts with a thorough wash, iron decontamination, and clay bar treatment. Ceramic coating bonds best to completely clean, contamination-free paint.

03

Paint Prep / Correction

We inspect the paint in detail. Swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation are corrected by machine polishing before any coating goes on. What goes under, stays under.

04

Coating & Cure

Pro-grade 9H ceramic is applied panel by panel in a controlled environment. We cure, inspect, and deliver your car with a thorough explanation of aftercare. Done right or not at all.

FAQs.

Do you offer mobile ceramic coating across Melbourne?
Yes. Shelby Ceramic Coating offers a fully mobile service and will come to your home, workplace, or any convenient location across Melbourne and surrounding suburbs. We also have indoor studio facilities in Vermont and Springvale for drop-off appointments where the controlled environment allows for the highest quality coating application.
How quickly do you respond to quote requests?
We respond to every enquiry within 30 minutes during business hours — this is our personal guarantee. Call 0490 699 614 or submit the online form. Shelby will be in contact within half an hour to discuss your vehicle and provide a quote.
How long does ceramic coating last in Melbourne?
A professionally applied 9H ceramic coating in Melbourne typically lasts 3 to 7 years depending on product grade, paint preparation quality, and your maintenance routine. Melbourne's UV, heat, and road conditions are exactly what ceramic coating is designed to combat. We use professional-grade products and thorough preparation for maximum longevity.
Does ceramic coating make my car scratch-proof?
Ceramic coating does not make your car scratch-proof, but it provides significantly better scratch resistance than bare paint or wax. The 9H hardness rating helps resist light swirl marks from washing and minor surface abrasion. It will not prevent deep scratches from stones or physical damage.
Do I need paint correction before ceramic coating?
For any vehicle with existing swirl marks, scratches, or oxidation — yes, absolutely. Ceramic coating seals whatever is underneath it. If defects aren't removed first, they get locked in permanently under a hardened layer. For new or near-new cars, thorough decontamination and surface prep is usually sufficient without full correction. We assess every vehicle before recommending a path.
