Ceramic Coating Oakleigh — FAQ

Do I need to bring my car to you, or do you come to Oakleigh? +
We operate from our detailing studio in Clayton — 5 min drive from Oakleigh. Ceramic coating requires a controlled, dust-free environment for a streak-free, flawless application. Call Savvy on 0490 699 614 to arrange a drop-off time.
How long will the coating last on my car? +
With Melbourne's UV and climate, our Premium package provides 3–5 years on a daily driver. Ultimate extends to 7+ years. The key factors are correct prep, correct application, and avoiding automated car washes after coating.
Is ceramic coating worth it compared to waxing? +
Yes. A quality wax lasts 2–4 months in Melbourne's climate. A professional ceramic coat lasts years with no reapplication. Over 5 years, ceramic typically costs the same or less than repeated waxing — with dramatically better protection. Read our full ceramic vs wax guide here.
