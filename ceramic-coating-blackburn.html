Ceramic Coating for Blackburn Cars

Blackburn is one of Melbourne's most established eastern suburbs — its mature oak and elm-lined streets are iconic, but they're also one of the biggest sources of paint-damaging sap and organic fallout in the area. Blackburn cars face constant environmental attack from spring through autumn.

Why Blackburn Cars Need Ceramic Coating

Blackburn's established deciduous trees produce sap, tannins, and organic acids that etch into unprotected clear coat — particularly in summer when Melbourne's UV accelerates the etching process. Ceramic coating creates a chemical-resistant barrier that prevents these contaminants from bonding to your paint.
